Print Design
The roots of my personal design history
I first started designing in school news organizations and have worked with numerous entities for the past 6 years including the Spark Newsmagazine, Fisher Ink, and The Lantern Newspaper. During my print design career I have served in several editor and managing editor positions, most recently as the Managing Editor for Design for the Lantern Newspaper, the independent student voice at Ohio State University.
