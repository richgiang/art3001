ABOUT ME

Hi, I’m Richard Ton Giang (Viet name: Giang Gia Ton) and I’m a Vietnamese-American storyteller based out of Columbus Ohio that values the power of humble narratives. My background is in Human-Computer Interaction, Screenwriting, Design Thinking, and Journalism, but I believe that just scratches the surface of my interests and career direction. Above all, I’m someone who is always looking to tell stories that are humbly impactful as well as innovative and exciting ways to tell them.

I have a strong background in print layout design, photojournalism, web design, UX/UI design, and multiple forms of writing. What ties my diverse skills together is my innate drive to learn more and share my unique experiences with the rest of the world. I see myself as a catalyst that digs up inconspicuous narratives so others can empathize and learn from them as much as I have.

With this drive and experience I have collaborated with diverse teams to produce novel and compelling solutions to complex, wicked problems throughout my undergraduate career. My experiences, skills, and mantra of always finishing my food have provided me with that ability to propel any team forward while providing truly creative and thoughtful ideas. I seek to bring these qualities to undertake any problem, so let’s co-create inspiring and moving experiential solutions.
